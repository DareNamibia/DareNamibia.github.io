Turkish Airlines Now Lands Weekly in Windhoek

Posted by Alex Hue on 6 August 2020

Turkish Airlines have announced that they are now to land in Windhoek, as from the 10th August 2020, in Namibia on a weekly basis.

This is wonderful news for our clients as transit times for shipments to many destinations will be greatly reduced as a result.
